1. MON COMPTE CLIENT

You can perfectly place an order on our site without creating a customer account.

However, creating an account will give you access to several advantages, such as:
- Take advantage of our Private Sales
- Save your delivery addresses and order faster
- Track the status of your orders in real time
- Consult the history of your orders and renew an order in 1 click
If you are logged into your account when you browse our site, your Basket will also be automatically saved until your next visit.

Click on the “My Account” tab and complete the registration form before validating. An email will be automatically sent to you to confirm your registration.
All the information that you communicate to us via this form is strictly confidential and its use is exclusively reserved for our company, in order to ensure the management and delivery of your orders.
In accordance with the Data-processing law and Freedoms n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978, you have a right of access, rectification, opposition and suppression of the data concerning you near our company.

You can modify your personal information at any time by logging in via the “My Account” tab.
Then edit the fields of your choice and click on "Validate" to save your changes.

When creating your customer account, an email containing your password was sent to you.
If you can't find this email, just click on "Forgot your password?" when logging into your account.
A link to reset your password will then be automatically sent to the email address provided when you registered.

Subscribing to our Newsletter will allow you to be informed of all our offers and promotions. To subscribe, simply enter your email address on our Newsletter subscription page.
You can then unsubscribe from our Newsletter at any time:
- either by unchecking the “Newsletter subscription” box from your customer account
- either by clicking on the unsubscribe link present in all our newsletters
- either by sending your unsubscribe request to service.client@bysmaquillage.fr

2. ORDERS & TRACKING

When you view a product, you can add it to your Basket by clicking on the “Order” button.
You can then easily withdraw it or modify its quantity directly from the page of your Basket.
To finalize your order, go to your Basket and click on “Order”. Then follow the steps indicated: identification,
choice of address, choice of delivery method and payment.
Please note: at the time of the last “Payment” step, be sure to check the contents of your Basket before choosing a payment method and finalizing your purchase.
Once the payment method has been chosen and the order has been validated, it can no longer be modified.
After validating your order, you will receive a confirmation email summarizing your purchases as well as the delivery method and address chosen.
As soon as your order has been shipped, you will receive a second email containing the tracking number for your package.

As soon as you validate an order at the “Payment” stage, it is immediately recorded in our system to allow us to prepare and ship it as quickly as possible.
This is a fully automated computer processing that does not allow you to manually cancel or modify an order once it has been validated.
If necessary, we invite you to contact our Customer Service so that we can define with you the best possible solution, depending on your problem.

Our switchboard is open Monday to Friday, 9 a.m. to 12 p.m. and 2 p.m. to 4 p.m. Outside these hours or if you are unable to reach us due to a busy line, you can send us any request by email to the address service.client@bysmaquillage.fr We respond to all written requests within 24 hours (excluding weekends and public holidays). NB: Our switchboard does not have a voicemail. If you are unable to reach us immediately by telephone, please do not hesitate to contact us by email.

4.LIVRAISON

Upon receipt of your payment, we make every effort to ship your order as soon as possible. On weekdays, orders are generally prepared within 24 hours. Orders placed between Friday afternoon and Sunday evening are processed from the following Monday.
Once your order has been dispatched, delivery times then depend on the carrier chosen: 48 hours for Colissimo, 3 to 5 days for Mondial Relay. As soon as your package has been handed over to the carrier, we will send you a package tracking number by email allowing you to follow its delivery.
NB: In periods of strong promotions such as Black Friday or €1 Flash Sales, our preparation times may be slightly longer due to the influx of orders: we nevertheless strive to respect the shortest possible deadlines and are always strengthening our teams. for this type of operation.

First check the delivery address or the Relay Point indicated on your order confirmation email. We deliver exclusively to the address you chose when confirming your order.
You can also use the parcel tracking number that we sent to you by email to check the status of your parcel on the carrier's website. This will allow you to be notified in the event of a delay or malfunction on their part.
If after 14 days, you still have not received your order, please let us know by email (service.client@bysmaquillage.fr) indicating your order number. We will immediately initiate an investigation with the carrier to trace your package and complete its delivery.

From 49€ of purchase, you can take advantage of the free Mondial Relay delivery. A tip to quickly reach this level: place a group order with your friends;)
Several times a year, we also offer delivery during special operations. To be notified as soon as we launch a “free delivery” flash offer, subscribe to our newsletter!
